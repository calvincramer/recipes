## Ingredients
- 454g or 1 lb dry bread
- sausage
- onion
- garlic
- celery
- 3/4 cup butter 
- broth 2 1/2 cups (seems like a lot but its right)
- 2 eggs
- parsley, safe, thyme

## Steps
1. cut or tear bread into bite sized pieces, around 3/4"
2. if necessary bread dry in oven at low temperature
3. add bread to big bowl
4. brown sausage, add to bowl
5. saute butter, onion, celery, garlic, salt, pepper around 3-4 minutes, make sure celery is not too hard. May want to give head start to celery.
6. add in broth and spices to above, mix
7. add to the bowl, mix
8. salt to taste
9. add to casserole baking pan
10. bake 20-30 minutes at 300F
