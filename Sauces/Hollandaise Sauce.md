## Ingredients
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/2 cup butter, melted
- salt
- pepper
- cayenne

## Steps
- in a container for a hand blender, add egg yolks and lemon juice
- emulsify butter
- to taste add salt, pepper, cayenne, more lemon juice
