## Ingredients
- 114g or 1/2 cup meat drippings, sub butter
- 31g or 1/4 cup flour
- 478g or 2 cups broth or stock
- optionally finely diced onion and garlic

## Steps
1. make a rue with drippings and flour, brown until satisfactory
2. add onion / garlic if desired
3. add in broth, mix
4. salt and pepper to taste

## Notes
- lenient on everything. Just a thickened broth essentially.
