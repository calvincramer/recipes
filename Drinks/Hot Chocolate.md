## Ingredients 2 servings
- milk 2 cups
- cocoa powder 2 tbsp
- sugar 2 tbsp
- chocolate chips 1/4 cup
- vanilla extract 1/8 tsp

## Steps
1. heat milk medium heat
2. add cocoa powder and sugar
3. when hot add chocolate chips, stir until melted
4. add vanilla

## Notes
- don't forget the marshmallows!
