## Sauces
### Chinese Hoisin
- 125g hoisin sauce 
- 100g soy sauce 
- 15g sesame oil 
- 125g honey 
- 20g (4-5 cloves) garlic, minced 
- 20g fresh ginger, grated
1. combine everything
2. in a pot, bring to a simmer
3. reduce for 10 minutes
4. cool before putting on wings
