Makes a 9-inch pie

## Ingredients
Crust:
- 1 1/4 cup flour
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 8 tbsp cold butter cubed
- 4 tbsp ice-cold water

Filling:
- 15 oz pumpkin puree
- 3x eggs
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 1.5 tsp cinnamon 
- 0.5 tsp ginger
- 0.25 tsp clove
- 0.25 tsp salt
- 0.25 tsp allspice
- 0.25 tsp nutmeg

## Steps
Crust:
1. set oven to 375F
2. in food processor add flour, salt, sugar. Mix
3. add in cubed butter, mix
4. add cold water until dough just comes together
5. roll dough into 12 inch circle
6. put dough into pie dish
7. trim edges
8. put crust in fridge until ready with filling

Filling:
1. in bowl add all ingredients for the filling. Mix thoroughly.
2. add filling to unbaked crust
3. bake for 40 - 55 minutes
4. cool on wire rack for 3 hours
5. store in fridge for a least a few hours before serving
