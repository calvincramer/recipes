This is supposed to be a pumpkin pie flavored cheesecake.

Currently it is half-half ingredients-wise between [Cheesecake](Cheesecake.md) and [Pumpkin Pie](Pumpkin%20Pie.md). *It turned out to be too wet*. Next iteration should include more of the flavor of pumpkin pie (spices definitely) without so much liquid (milk, cream).

Use the crust from [Cheesecake](Cheesecake.md)

## Ingredients
Filling:
- 340g cream cheese
- 90g sugar
- 3 eggs
- 60g brown sugar
- 212g pumpkin puree
- 3g salt
- 5g vanilla
- 65g sour cream
- 20g cornstarch
- 90g heavy cream
- 61g milk
- 1.5 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp ginger
- 1/2 tsp nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp clove
- 1/4 tsp allspice
