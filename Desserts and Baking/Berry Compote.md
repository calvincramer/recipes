## Ingredients
- 450g berries (strawberry is good)
- 90g sugar
- 15g cornstarch, dissolved in some (26g) water
- lemon juice 25g
- 110g water

## Steps
1. add berries, lemon juice, sugar, water to pot
2. bring to boil on medium heat, stirring
3. blend 1/3, add back to pot
4. add cornstarch slurry, cook 1-2 min low heat
6. add lemon juice to taste
7. add more cornstarch slurry or just water to get to preferred thickness
